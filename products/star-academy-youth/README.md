# Star Academy Youth

All-ages card RPG product profile.

## Design Direction

- Bright sci-fantasy, optimistic tone, school-and-adventure framing.
- Safety-first social features with strict moderation defaults.
- Monetization with parental controls and anti-pressure UX.

## Gameplay Pillars

- Readable turn-based combat with short sessions.
- Companion and pet progression with clear rewards.
- Seasonal world-route events and cooperative-friendly goals.

## Content Rules

- Family-safe visual language only.
- No off-platform contact nudges.
- Youth-safe chat and community guardrails.
