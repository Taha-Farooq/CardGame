# Astral Rift Rivals

Mature-audience (non-explicit) card RPG product profile.

## Design Direction

- Darker techno-fantasy tone and higher combat complexity.
- Distinct visual system, UI language, and faction identity from youth product.
- Competitive systems centered on mastery and build expression.

## Gameplay Pillars

- Tactical status-engine interactions and counterplay.
- Ranked seasonal structure with transparent balancing notes.
- Cosmetic economy and prestige progression loops.

## Content Rules

- Mature themes allowed, explicit sexual content excluded.
- Separate branding, channels, and legal/age-gate experience.
- No cross-funnel UX from youth-facing products.
