# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-26

This Privacy Policy explains how Arcane Star Academy ("Game") operated by Existensial Prism Studios ("Operator", "we", "us") handles information.

## 1. Information We Collect

Depending on deployment stage, we may collect:

- account identifiers (username, email, platform ID);
- gameplay telemetry (progression, purchases, session events);
- device and technical data (IP, browser/device type, crash logs);
- support communications.

In the current local prototype, progress is stored in local browser storage on the user's device.

## 2. How We Use Data

We use data to:

- operate and secure the Game;
- process purchases and manage virtual entitlements;
- improve gameplay balance and performance;
- provide support and enforce policies.

## 3. Legal Bases and Consent

Where required, we process data based on consent, contract performance, legitimate interests, or legal obligations.

## 4. Children and Family Use

The Game is designed to be age-appropriate, but monetization and account functions should be supervised by parents/guardians. We recommend parental controls and spending limits.

## 5. Sharing

We may share data with:

- hosting, analytics, payment, and support providers;
- legal authorities when required by law.

We do not sell personal data.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for service operation, legal compliance, and dispute resolution.

## 7. Security

We use reasonable safeguards but cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Your Rights

Subject to local law, users may request access, correction, deletion, portability, or restriction of personal data processing.

## 9. International Transfers

Data may be processed in countries other than your own, with appropriate safeguards where required.
